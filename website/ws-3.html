International Students Accomodation

All rooms are furnished and DMU provides a welcome pack that includes a duvet, pillow and bedsheets. See the links below for halls available:

  • Filbert Village
  • Newarke Point
  • The Grange
  • Liberty Court (formerly Victoria Hall)
  • Liberty Park
  • New Wharf Hall
    • The Glassworks
  • St Martins House

September 2017 intake

Accommodation is guaranteed for international students who accept an offer to study at DMU by Monday 24th July. This guarantee is open to students arriving in September 2017 only. Find out more about our International Student Accommodation Guarantee

Accomodation for English Language Courses

For international students coming to study the English Language Preparation Programme (ELPP) and/or Pre-sessional English course after June 2017, more information will be announced on this page soon.Typically accommodation is available at Liberty Court (formerly Victoria Hall) and Liberty Park.

Returning Students

DMU aims to provide a room for all returning international students upon request (subject to availability). Returning overseas and EU students will have the same choice of halls as UK students.
