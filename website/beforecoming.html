Preparation and Arrival

Travelling to any international destination for studying is challenging. De Montfort University attracts candidates from countries in an out of the European Union. We are committed to provide all sort of help and guidance to our international students before they come to the UK.

DMU International offices

Having the vision of becoming a global golbal university, DMU acquires offices at various international destinations. DMU welcomes around 2700 international students every year from around 130 countries and aims to provide all information to candiates according tho their home country.
