Opening a bank account

To open a bank account in the UK, you need to provide your identity proofs and there are different types of bank accounts that bank provides. Basic bank accounts are easier to open than any other type of account but, nevertheless, it can take a long time to open one. DMU will give you, or have already given you, an enrolment letter. You should keep the original enrolment letter in a safe place, for opening a bank account.

Follow these simple steps to open a bank account in Leicester

  1. Arrive in the leicester and enrol at DMU

  2. Get a letter which states that you are a student at De Montfort University. This letter can be tailored and addressed to the bank of your choice if you ask for it.

  3. Get your documents ready- Passport, British residence permit, Enrolment letter

  4. Go to the bank of your choice(List of nearest banks is given below on this page)

  5. Book and appointment for opening a bank account. You may be able to get an appoitment for same day.

  6. Sign at the relevant documents and wait for few working days to get your debit card.
