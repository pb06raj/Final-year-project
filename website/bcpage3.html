September 2017 intake

Accommodation is guaranteed for international students who accept an offer to study at DMU by Monday 24th July. This guarantee is open to students arriving in September 2017 only. Find out more about our International Student Accommodation Guarantee

Important information- When you will enter in the UK, you have to provide an address that where you will be going from airport. So, make sure you have sorted out your accomodation beforehand.
