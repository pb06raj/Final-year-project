Initial settiling at DMU

De Montfort University is committed to provide every possible facility to help International Students in settling when they arrive here. Some of the services that DMU provides are given below:

Buddy Scheme

By coming to De Montfort University (DMU) you are embarking on an exciting and life-changing adventure, but you may still need some help along the way.

Coming to live in a new country is often exciting, though at first it can seem challenging or even a little stressful. Things can seem different from what you are used to in your own country, particularly if English is not your first language.

The aim of the Buddy scheme is to match newly arrived international students with existing DMU students who have already been here for a year or more. We hope that the existing student Buddies will be able to help you to make new friends, show you the city and settle into your new life in Leicester.

How can you register?

You can register for the Buddy scheme online here.

Once you have registered, we will get in touch with you with more information about the scheme and about meeting your buddy.

If you have any questions, you can contact us directly at i-buddies@dmu.ac.uk.

HOST UK for international students

All EU and international students are being given a great opportunity to experience more of the UK with national charity HOST UK, founded by the British Council and the Foreign and Commonwealth Office in 1987. Students will be able to enjoy a day, weekend or even Christmas with a friendly individual or family based in the UK, and approved by HOST.
